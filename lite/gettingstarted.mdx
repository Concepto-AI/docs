## Signing In
<Steps>
    <Step>
        To sign in, visit lite.conceptoai.app, and you'll be automatically redirected to the authentication page.
    </Step>
    <Step>
        If it's your first time, click on `Create an Account`, and either use Social Login (Google, Github, etc.) or use your email to create an account.
        <Warning>You must use the email you gave in the `OAuth Email Address` field when you bought your subscription. If you used the wrong email, please get in touch with us.</Warning>
    </Step>
    <Step>
        If you use Social Login, the **primary** email on the account must be the email you gave during checkout. See above if you made a whoopsie.
    </Step>
    <Step>
        If you get redirected to a page which says `You don't have permission to access this page`, or similar, your subscription hasn't been activated yet. Go ahead and contact us to get help!
    </Step>
    <Step>
        If it all works, you should find yourself at the canvas page. Read on to find out how to start designing!
    </Step>
</Steps>

## Start Designing

Using the intuitive `tldraw` whiteboard design tools, craft your website.

<Tip>
  You can include arrows pointing to specific areas of your project with helpful hints for the AI to code.
</Tip>

If you get stuck, have a look online at `tldraw` tutorials, or send us a message!

## Concepto it

Once you've perfected your design,  add your API key in the box which says `Your OpenAI Key` hit `Concepto it` in the bottom right hand corner. This will start loading, and soon you'll be presented with a view for code, and a view for preview.

<Tip>Still need help? Get in touch with us</Tip>
